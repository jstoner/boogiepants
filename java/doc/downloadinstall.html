installing

Mac OSX platform

  1. Open the .dmg file;
  2. copy the boogiepants app to your Applications;
  3. copy the boogiepants directory to your home directory, or someplace easy to find.
  4. install Pure Data. Choose the right version of pd-extended for the mac.

initial exploration

  1. start up the boogiepants program. You should see this screen:

  2. Follow the instructions there. Getting the connection can take a few tries. Patience.
  3. Once we have a good connection, the display window will come up. You will see two sticks. The top one moves with the nunchuk, the bottom one with the wii remote.
  4. Go into File > Open, and go into boogiepants/resources, and open example.pants.
  5. Start Pure Data, and go into File > Open. Go into boogiepants/pd. Open the file named oscserv.pd. Go to the console of Pure Data and make sure the 'compute audio' checkbox is checked.
  6. If you don't have a garment to put them in, just hold the wii remote against your chest, buttons out, and the nunchuk against your pelvis, pointing down.
  7. Play with it! Press various buttons, on the wii remote, and see what effects you hear. Play with the centering button and sensitivity on the console.
