the types of instruments

the strike instrument

the strike instrument is pretty simple--when you hit it with the stick, it plays.

In the example.pants file, the 1 and 2 wiimote buttons bring up sets of strike instruments.

needs video

the scale instrument

the scale instrument shows two spheres--a pink one and a blue one. When the stick intersects the line between the center of the two spheres, it plays. It plays in a way that depends on which sphere it's closer to.

In the example.pants file, the '-' wiimote button brings up a scale instrument.

needs video

the pelvic circle instrument

The pelvic circle instrument shows a cone at the bottom and a ring with a marker near the stick. It plays depending on the radial angle of the stick with the cone. The 'a' and left buttons in example.pants bring up pelvic circle instruments.

needs video

the toggle instrument

The toggle instruments shows a cone at the bottom of the screen. It starts and stops playing when you push its button. The 'down' button on the remote shows a toggle instrument in example.pants.
